## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Click "Sign Up" in the navigation bar
   - Or visit `/register`

2. **Choose Your Role**
   - **Buyer**: If you want to purchase services
   - **Seller**: If you want to offer services
   - You can switch roles later in settings

3. **Fill in Your Details**
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Secure Password
   - Or sign up with Google

4. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for verification email
   - Click the verification link

### Logging In

1. **Email & Password**
   - Enter your credentials
   - Click "Sign In"

2. **Google Sign-In**
   - Click "Continue with Google"
   - Select your Google account

## For Buyers

### Finding Services

1. **Browse Marketplace**
   - Visit `/marketplace`
   - Use category filters
   - Apply price range filters
   - Sort by price, rating, or newest

2. **Search**
   - Use the search bar in navigation
   - Get instant suggestions
   - Click on suggestions or press Enter

3. **View Gig Details**
   - Click on any gig card
   - Review seller profile
   - Check packages and pricing
   - Read reviews from other buyers

### Placing an Order

1. **Select a Package**
   - Choose Basic, Standard, or Premium
   - Review what's included
   - Check delivery time

2. **Provide Requirements**
   - Fill in project details
   - Upload reference files if needed
   - Be specific about your needs

3. **Checkout**
   - Review order summary
   - Enter payment details (Stripe)
   - Confirm purchase

4. **Track Your Order**
   - Go to `/dashboard/orders`
   - View order status
   - Check delivery date
   - Message the seller

### Managing Orders

**Order Statuses:**
- **Pending**: Payment processing
- **In Progress**: Seller is working
- **Delivered**: Work submitted for review
- **Completed**: Order finished
- **Disputed**: Issue reported

**Actions You Can Take:**
- **Message Seller**: Ask questions, provide feedback
- **Accept Delivery**: Mark order as complete
- **Request Revision**: If work needs changes
- **Report Problem**: Open a dispute

### Leaving Reviews

1. **After Order Completion**
   - Go to order details
   - Click "Leave a Review"

2. **Rate Your Experience**
   - Select 1-5 stars
   - Write detailed feedback
   - Be honest and constructive

3. **Review Guidelines**
   - Focus on the service quality
   - Mention communication
   - Note delivery time
   - Be respectful

## For Sellers

### Creating Your First Gig

1. **Go to Create Gig**
   - Click "Sell" in navigation
   - Or visit `/create-gig`

2. **Fill in Gig Details**
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive (max 80 chars)
   - **Category**: Choose relevant category
   - **Description**: Detailed service description
   - **Images**: Upload 3-5 high-quality images
   - **Tags**: Add relevant keywords

3. **Set Up Packages**
   - **Basic**: Entry-level offering
   - **Standard**: Mid-tier with more features
   - **Premium**: Complete package

   For each package, specify:
   - Price
   - Delivery time
   - Features included
   - Number of revisions

4. **Add FAQs** (Optional)
   - Answer common questions
   - Reduce buyer inquiries

5. **Publish Your Gig**
   - Review all details
   - Click "Publish"
   - Gig goes live immediately

### Managing Orders

1. **New Order Notification**
   - Email notification
   - In-app notification
   - Check `/dashboard/orders`

2. **Review Requirements**
   - Read buyer's requirements carefully
   - Ask clarifying questions if needed
   - Start work promptly

3. **Update Order Status**
   - Mark as "In Progress" when starting
   - Keep buyer updated
   - Deliver on time

4. **Deliver Work**
   - Upload final files
   - Add delivery message
   - Click "Deliver Work"

5. **Handle Revisions**
   - Review buyer feedback
   - Make requested changes
   - Re-deliver updated work

### Best Practices for Sellers

**Profile Optimization:**
- Professional profile photo
- Complete bio
- Showcase your skills
- Add portfolio samples

**Communication:**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Be professional and friendly
- Set clear expectations
- Update buyers regularly

**Quality:**
- Deliver high-quality work
- Meet deadlines
- Exceed expectations
- Ask for feedback

**Pricing:**
- Research competitors
- Price competitively
- Offer value at each tier
- Update prices as you grow

## Payment & Orders

### Payment Methods

**Supported:**
- Credit/Debit Cards (via Stripe)
- Google Pay
- Apple Pay

**Coming Soon:**
- PayPal
- Bank Transfer

### Order Process

1. **Checkout**: Secure payment via Stripe
2. **Payment Hold**: Funds held in escrow
3. **Work Delivery**: Seller completes work
4. **Buyer Approval**: You review and accept
5. **Payment Release**: Seller receives payment

### Refunds & Disputes

**When to Open a Dispute:**
- Work not delivered
- Quality issues
- Seller unresponsive
- Work doesn't match description

**Dispute Process:**
1. Click "Report Problem" on order
2. Select reason
3. Provide detailed description
4. Admin reviews case
5. Resolution within 5-7 business days

**Refund Policy:**
- Full refund if work not delivered
- Partial refund for quality issues
- No refund after accepting delivery

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is GigStream free to use?**
A: Yes! Creating an account and browsing is free. We charge a 10% service fee on completed orders.

**Q: How do I become a seller?**
A: Create an account, complete your profile, and publish your first gig!

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit/debit cards via Stripe, plus Google Pay and Apple Pay.

**Q: How long does it take to get paid?**
A: Sellers receive payment 14 days after order completion or immediately after buyer approval.

### Buyer Questions

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the work?**
A: Request revisions (if included in package) or open a dispute for a refund.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Yes, before the seller starts work. Contact the seller or support.

**Q: How do I know if a seller is reliable?**
A: Check their ratings, reviews, response time, and completion rate.

### Seller Questions

**Q: How much can I earn?**
A: Unlimited! Your earnings depend on your pricing, quality, and number of orders.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: 14 days after order completion, or immediately when buyer marks as complete.

**Q: Can I offer custom packages?**
A: Yes! Use the messaging system to discuss custom requirements and pricing.

**Q: What if a buyer is difficult?**
A: Stay professional, document everything, and contact support if needed.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes! We use Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We never store card details.

**Q: Can I use GigStream on mobile?**
A: Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: What file types can I upload?**
A: Images (JPG, PNG, GIF), Documents (PDF, DOC), and more. Max 10MB per file.
